ABOUT ME

I possess an entrepreneurial mindset with a strong passion to create technologies that enable people and businesses to grow! This mindset has recently led me to some of my most rewarding yet challenging journeys ever.

I firmly believe in taking on new challenges and capitalizing on opportunities of growth. Thus, in the summer of 2020, amidst the pandemic, I had co-founded a virtual restaurant named Khao Jee to cash in on the growing demand for food delivery in my city.

As the co-founder of a restaurant, I had personally experienced the challenges a (start-up) restaurant faces in inventory control, maintaining purchase logs and producing income summaries.

These challenges had re-ignited my passion of creating impactful technologies. Following my passion, I learned the Django Framework by taking Codecademy's Django Course.
Using the newly acquired knowledge, I developed RestManagement - a full-stack web application designed to help manage restaurants efficiently by streamlining the operations of inventory control, maintaining purchase logs and producing income summaries, which can reduce costs by up to 25%!


Over the last year, I have developed a keen interest in the world of personal finances, particularly in stock trading. With my growing interest, I had explored various resources for proper guidance in this risky venture. Aiming to further strengthen my understanding of the mechanics of trading, I integrated my passion of creating impactful technologies with my interest in trading stocks to build Trade $tock - a full-stack web application developed with Flask that simulates the real-life workings of the equities market for users to build their virtual trading portfolio based on real-time stock data.

The main goal of my app was to engage users, like myself, in the world of economics, investing and personal finance by providing a platform where stocks can be traded firsthand in real-world settings to build a portfolio, without any financial risk.
